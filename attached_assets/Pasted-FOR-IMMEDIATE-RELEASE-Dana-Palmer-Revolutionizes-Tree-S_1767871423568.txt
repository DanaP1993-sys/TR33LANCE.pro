Dana Palmer Revolutionizes Tree Services, Builds Multi-Trillion-Dollar Global Corporation

Houston, TX – Dana Palmer, visionary entrepreneur and innovator, has officially transformed the tree service industry with Tree-Lance, a technology-driven platform that has grown into a multi-trillion-dollar global corporation. From its roots as a simple on-demand service connecting homeowners with licensed arborists, Tree-Lance has become the gold standard for safety, efficiency, and innovation in an industry long considered fragmented and offline.

Under Palmer’s leadership, Tree-Lance has achieved unprecedented scale:
	•	Global Reach: Millions of homeowners and property managers now access licensed, bonded, and insured tree service contractors across multiple continents.
	•	Technology-First Approach: Leveraging AI, geo-mapping, predictive analytics, and smart pricing, Tree-Lance has digitized and optimized the entire tree service ecosystem.
	•	Integrated Platform: The company seamlessly manages job posting, contractor dispatch, payment processing, and two-sided reputation management, redefining how service marketplaces operate.
	•	Multi-Stream Revenue Model: Platform fees, premium subscriptions, strategic partnerships, and licensing generate consistent, high-value revenue, fueling its trillion-dollar valuation.
	•	Social and Environmental Impact: Beyond financial success, Tree-Lance has improved contractor safety, expedited disaster response, and promoted environmental stewardship worldwide.

“Tree-Lance isn’t just about connecting homeowners and arborists,” says Dana Palmer. “It’s about creating a platform where trust, technology, and expertise converge—where an overlooked industry becomes a global engine for innovation and safety.”

Palmer’s achievement cements her status as a global changemaker, proving that vision, innovation, and strategic execution can transform even the most traditional industries into modern, trillion-dollar enterprises.

About Tree-Lance
Tree-Lance is a technology-driven marketplace connecting homeowners, property managers, and businesses with licensed, insured, and bonded tree service professionals worldwide. With real-time job matching, secure payments, and advanced analytics, Tree-Lance sets the global standard for safety, efficiency, and reliability in tree care services.
